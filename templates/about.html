BRAINYBOT Your Personal AI-powered Smart Study Assistant

How Can I Help You Today?

  • What is BrainyBot?

    MK

  • BrainyBot is an intelligent, AI-powered web application designed to assist students in their academic journey. Built using state-of-the-art machine learning and natural language processing technologies, BrainyBot enables users to interact with their study materials in smarter, more meaningful ways.

    It's your personal academic assistant — whether you’re preparing for exams, revising concepts, or organizing learning resources.

  • What are the Features of BrainyBot?

    MK

  • Our key features include:

    Ask Complex Questions :
    Ask any question, and BrainyBot searches the web, understands your intent, and returns concise, informative answers.
    Chat With PDF :
    Upload your notes, textbooks, or study papers as PDFs. Simply ask questions based on the content and get clear, context-aware answers.
    Chat with YouTube :
    Paste a YouTube video link, and BrainyBot transcribes and understands the content so you can ask questions based on the video.
    Notes Generator :
    Summarizes large blocks of text or lecture transcripts into concise summaries — perfect for quick revisions.
    MCQ Generator :
    Automatically generates multiple-choice questions based on study material (PDF) — great for self-assessment or quiz creation.
    Flashcard Generator :
    Create flashcards from PDFs, summaries, or videos to help with quick and repeated recall of important terms and definitions.

  • Why should i choose BrainyBot?

    • Smart: Uses trained models for truly intelligent responses.

  • What are the Use-Cases of BrainyBot?

    MK

  • Our key use-cases include:

    • Exam Preparation: Get quick answers to complex questions, helping you prepare for exams efficiently.
    • Research Assistance: BrainyBot can help you find relevant information and summarize research papers or articles.
    • Lecture Review: Upload lecture notes or videos and ask questions to clarify concepts.
    • Personal Revision Planning: BrainyBot can help you create a personalized study plan based on your learning style and pace.
    • MCQ Practice & Flashcards for Revision: Generate multiple-choice questions and flashcards from your study material for effective revision.
